About Me

Hello World! After graduating UMass Amherst with a degree in communications and Japanese in 2008, I was pretty much fucked. I have held an extremely wide range of positions - from waitressing to technical support to medical billing to struggling actress. Back when I graduated, I was eager to join the workforce and put off going back to school until I found a subject that I was truly passionate about.

Web design and front-end coding has always been a hobby of mine. At 13 years old I created my first website with HTML. At 15 I was president of my High School's Technology’s Project where I helped designed websites for local artists. When I decided to go back to school it all became clear that web design and development would be the right path to take in order to obtain a rewarding career. Now that I have immersed myself in developing the web, I can not imagine doing anything else.
